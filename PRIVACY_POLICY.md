## 3. Why we collect it (purpose of processing)

- **To operate the marketplace**: matching workers with shifts, enabling bids/offers, generating and storing digital work contracts, and facilitating in-app chat between a worker and the employer they're engaged with.
- **To verify identity (KYC)**: as required for trust and safety on the Platform, and as permitted under the National Registration Act 1959 (Act 78).
- **To process payments**: calculating and paying out wages owed to Workers, and billing Employers.
- **To meet legal obligations**: record-keeping required under the Employment Act 1955 (Act 265), the Gig Workers Act 2025 (Act 872), and tax/audit requirements.
- **To communicate with you**: sending notifications about your bids, offers, shift status, and account activity (email and in-app).
- **To keep the Platform safe**: detecting fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.

We do not use your personal data for third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal data.

## 4. Who we share your data with

- **The other party to a shift.** If you're a Worker, an Employer who has engaged you for a shift can see your name, relevant profile/KYC status, and the contents of your chat with them. If you're an Employer, Workers who apply to or are engaged for your shifts can see your business name and chat with you.
- **Service providers who process data on our behalf**, under contract, strictly for the purposes above:
  - **Supabase** (database, authentication, file storage, and real-time messaging infrastructure)
  - **Resend** (transactional email delivery for notifications)
  - [Payment processor — to be confirmed once finalized]
  - **GitHub Pages** (hosting the web app's static files)
- **Government authorities**, where required by law (e.g. tax authorities, labor authorities, or in response to a valid legal request).

We do not disclose your personal data to any other third party for their own marketing purposes.

## 5. International data transfers

Some of our service providers (e.g. Supabase, Resend) may process or store data on servers located outside Malaysia. Where this happens, we take reasonable steps to ensure a comparable standard of protection is applied, as required under the PDPA's cross-border data transfer restrictions (Section 129).

## 6. How long we keep your data

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active, and for a minimum of **seven (7) years** after your last transaction on the Platform, to meet legal, tax, and audit obligations (particularly for KYC and payment records). If you close your account, some data may be retained for this statutory period even though your account is no longer active.

## 7. Security

We apply access controls (including database-level row security so users can generally only read their own data or data relevant to their shifts) and encryption in transit (HTTPS) to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data involved (especially identity documents and payment details).

## 8. Your rights under the PDPA

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate or outdated personal data.
- **Withdraw consent** for processing (though this may limit or end your ability to use the Platform, since some data is required for KYC/legal compliance).
- **Request deletion** of your account and associated data, subject to our statutory retention obligations (Section 6).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **support@carigaji.my**. We will respond within **fourteen (14) business days**, as required under the PDPA.

## 9. Cookies and local storage

The Platform uses browser local storage (not third-party tracking cookies) to remember your theme preference (light/dark) and language preference (English/Bahasa Melayu) on your device. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers as tracking data.

## 10. Children's privacy

The Platform is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
