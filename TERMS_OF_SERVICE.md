**CariGaji is a marketplace intermediary, not an employer.** We provide the technology that lets Workers discover and bid on shifts, and lets Employers post shifts and select Workers. We are not a party to the working arrangement agreed between a Worker and an Employer, and we are not the employer of any Worker who uses the Platform.

## 4. How the Platform works

1. **Posting a shift.** Employers post a shift with a role description, category, date/time, location, wage range, headcount, and any requirements.
2. **Bidding.** Workers browse open shifts and submit a bid (a proposed hourly wage within or near the Employer's stated range).
3. **Selection.** The Employer reviews the applicant pool and selects Worker(s) to fill the shift's headcount, up to the number of open positions remaining.
4. **Offer and confirmation.** A selected Worker receives an offer with a response deadline (scaled to how soon the shift starts) and must confirm or decline. If a Worker doesn't respond in time, the offer expires and the Employer may select another applicant.
5. **Digital contract.** Once a Worker confirms, both parties sign a digital work contract in-app covering the shift's terms (role, date, time, location, agreed wage).
6. **Completion and payment.** After the shift, payment is processed according to the agreed wage and the Platform's payout schedule (see Section 6).
